arginine

our new webcomic is here!!

arginine follows three idiots named emoti, ella, and leaf0n as they go about their completely normal and pretty average lives in the abandoned city of old york. they encounter the expected city folk and wild forest animals, such as garden gnomes and kurt cobain.

currently, the comic is being illustrated by plague, though artists will probably swap around a bit throughout.

additionally, we have launched a second webcomic called grinnaie which will contain short one-off stories and comics set in the arginine universe. this one will update significantly more frequently alongside (hopefully) having more artists outside of me, plague, and leaf0n.
